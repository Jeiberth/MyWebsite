Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22/02/2025

Thank you for using Trueque! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app. By using Trueque, you agree to the terms outlined below. If you disagree, please discontinue use.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Personal Information: Name, email, password, profile picture, and manually entered location (during registration)
  • Product/Service Information: Name, description, color, weight, price, category, material, and images
  • Chat Information: Text messages, timestamps, sender/recipient details, and read status
  • Offers/Ratings: Products/services/money exchanged, ratings (1-5), comments, and timestamps

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Location Data: Only collected via manual input (e.g., during registration). We do not track your device's GPS or precise location
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage logs collected

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable core features (profile creation, product listings, and exchanges)
  • Facilitate discovery of nearby products/services
  • Power real-time communication between users
  • Send verification codes, password resets, and policy updates
  • Monitor and prevent unauthorized access

We Do NOT:
- Sell, trade, or rent your data
- Use your data for advertising, marketing, or analytics

4. Data Sharing & Your Rights

4.1 No Sale/Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with external parties except as necessary for app functionality (see Section 3).

4.2 Your Control

  • Access/Update: Edit your profile/data in-app or email truequeww@gmail.com
  • Deletion: If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:
    1. Send an Email: To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to truequeww@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
    2. Data Deletion: Once your request is processed, all account-related data will be permanently deleted, except for any data that must be retained due to legal requirements (e.g., transaction history, tax records).
    3. Confirmation & Timeframe: You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours, and your account will be deleted within 7 business days.
    For more details, please visit our Account Deletion Request Page.

CCPA/GDPR Rights

  • Right to know what personal data is collected
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (not applicable as we don't sell data)

Submit requests via email. We respond within 30 days and verify identity before processing.

5. Data Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt algorithm
  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Images stored on AWS S3 with encryption at rest

6. Children’s Privacy

Trueque is not intended for users under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. To report underage users or harmful content:

  • Use the in-app chat (tap Truequi’s icon and describe the issue).
  • Email truequeww@gmail.com.

For details on how we protect minors, review our Child Safety Standards.
