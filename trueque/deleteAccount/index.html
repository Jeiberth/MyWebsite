Account Deletion Request

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  • 1.
    Send an Email:To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to truequeww@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  • 2.
    Data Deletion:Once your request is processed, all account-related data will be permanently deleted, except for any data that must be retained due to legal requirements (e.g., transaction history, tax records).
  • 3.
    Confirmation & Timeframe:You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours, and your account will be deleted within 7 business days.
