Trueque Safety Standards & Child Protection Policy

1. Introduction

Trueque is committed to fostering a safe and respectful environment for all users. We adhere to Google Play’s policies on child safety and strictly prohibit content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors. This document outlines our safety practices, reporting mechanisms, and compliance with child protection laws.

2. Safety Measures

Age Restrictions

  • Trueque is not intended for users under 13. By using the app, users confirm they are at least 13 years old.
  • We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect data from minors under 13.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

  • Sexualized, exploitative, or harmful material involving minors.
  • Content promoting illegal activities, including child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Explicit or adult-themed items/services.
  • Harassment, bullying, or predatory behavior.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Email Reporting Process

  • Users may report concerns directly via truequeww@gmail.com (dedicated to safety issues).

What to Include:

  • Screenshots or descriptions of the violating content/user.
  • Relevant details (username, item/service ID, timestamp).

Response Protocol:

  • Review: Reports are investigated within 24 hours by the Trueque team.
  • Action: Violations result in immediate content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans.
  • Escalation: Suspected CSAM or exploitation is reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement.

4. Compliance with Legal Obligations

  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We fully cooperate with authorities in CSAE investigations.
  • NCMEC Reporting: All CSAM incidents are reported to NCMEC via their CyberTipline.
  • Data Retention: User data related to investigations is preserved for legal purposes.

5. User Accountability & Education

  • Users agree to our Community Guidelines during sign-up, which prohibit illegal or harmful content.
  • Safety tips and guidelines are provided via email upon request (contact truequeww@gmail.com).

6. Ongoing Commitment

  • Policy Updates: This document is reviewed regularly to align with evolving laws and platform standards.
  • User Empowerment: We encourage users to report suspicious activity and block abusive accounts.
